research // verifiable systems

Build AI that can show its work.

My research connects compliance reasoning, retrieval, citation grounding, runtime verification, and secure system migration.

current thesis

I design systems for high-stakes questions where a plausible answer is not enough. The evidence, version, and reasoning path should remain inspectable by the people responsible for the decision.

01 / COMPLIANCEGPT

Version-aware compliance RAG

Decouples evidence selection from answer assembly to reduce version blindness, citation errors, and terminology hallucinations.

02 / EVIDENCE

Canonical clause retrieval

Parses NIST OSCAL catalogs into auditor-verifiable clauses, then combines hybrid retrieval stages for traceable answers.

03 / ZERO TRUST

Migration without a clean slate

Studies overlay-based paths for moving legacy infrastructure toward Zero Trust without requiring a full system replacement.
