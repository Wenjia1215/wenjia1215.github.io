Research

Verifiable AI for compliance question answering.

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. candidate at Florida International University working on AI systems that make generated answers easier to verify. My current dissertation work centers on ComplianceGPT, citation grounding, retrieval evidence, and runtime checking.

Clause evidence

Recover relevant governing clauses from compliance frameworks and preserve revision-specific evidence boundaries.

Contract assembly

Use explicit answer contracts to constrain how retrieved evidence is converted into final compliance answers.

Runtime verification

Check that final answers satisfy citation, support, and organization-defined-parameter constraints.
