Research agenda

Compliance answers should be checkable, not merely fluent.

My dissertation direction, ComplianceGPT, studies how compliance question answering can separate evidence selection from answer realization. The larger agenda is trustworthy AI for compliance, governance, audit, and other high-stakes question-answering settings.

Evidence recovery

Retrieve governing clauses while preserving source boundaries, document versions, and the specific evidence that supports each claim.

Answer contracts

Represent what an answer is allowed to claim before final text generation, reducing unsupported conclusions and vague citation behavior.

Runtime checking

Check citations, support status, policy constraints, and organization-defined values before accepting an AI-generated answer.
